About

I am a procurement and Supply chain Major. What inspired me to do this project is to improve the upstream and downstream of goods and services in the supply chain. I aim to add a lot of features to meet my objectives. This portfolio project handles a simple inventory management system where one can order goods, see what products are available, and visualize the goods.
